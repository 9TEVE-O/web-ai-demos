Safe Intake

The interface dissolves. The effect remains. The evidence survives.

A phone-first, no-backend proof for controlled AI intake. The capsule detects risk, blocks raw sensitive input, creates a reviewed redacted copy, produces an AI-visible summary, exports an evidence receipt, then dissolves its own working interface.

Doctrine

Destroy the capability. Preserve the accountability.

ASPRON is not just an app. It is a controlled lifecycle for temporary AI capability: activate, act, prove, revoke.

Layer 1 — Capsules

Single-purpose workflows for high-stakes jobs. Each capsule performs one verb and then revokes its own working surface.

Layer 2 — Pattern

detect → block → redact → approve → summarise → audit → dissolve. The pattern can become SDK infrastructure.

Layer 3 — Governance

ASPRON shows controlled intake. LAB audits reveal what enters, what is blocked, who approved it, and what evidence survives.
